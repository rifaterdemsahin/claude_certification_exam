🚀 Quick Start Guide

How to Use This App

  • 🃏 Memory Cards - Visual flashcards with hints and mnemonics. Click to flip!
  • 📝 Quiz - Interactive quiz with immediate feedback and scoring
  • 🧠 Remember - Quick flashcard review for rapid recall practice
  • 📋 Exam - Mock exam with 20 random questions and detailed results
  • 🎯 Mastery - Track your progress through all 100 questions
  • 📊 Analyse - Review performance statistics and weak areas
  • 🔍 Evaluate - Visual concept maps and architecture diagrams
  • 📚 Resources - Curated learning materials and videos
  • 🎮 Multiplayer - Quiz with friends (requires MQTT connection)

✅ Readiness Checklist

Do not book the exam until all of these are true. The exam costs money and has a cooldown between retakes.

☐
All 100 Memory Cards Mastered Every card in the app shows the green "Mastered" badge. No gaps.
☐
Mock Exam Passed Twice in a Row 85%+ on the in-app mock exam, two consecutive attempts, on different days.
☐
Weak Categories Fixed Any category below 80% mastery gets re-studied with targeted cards and concepts.
☐
Explain Any Card to a Friend If you can teach every card out loud without reading, you truly know it.
☐
One Day Rest Before Exam No new material. Light review of memory palaces only. Sleep > cramming.

📊 Score Thresholds

<70% Not ready. Go back to memory cards and concept deep-dives.
70-84% Close, but risky. Focus on weak categories and retake mock exam.
85-92% Good to book. One final review pass of all cards recommended.
93%+ Very strong. Book with confidence. Maintain with light spaced review.

🧠 Multi-Sensory Training

The brain encodes memories more deeply when multiple senses are engaged. Do not just read — see, hear, speak, and move.

👁️
Visual

Memory card images, mindmap, mermaid diagrams, color-coded categories.

👂
Auditory

Read cards out loud. Record yourself explaining concepts. Listen back.

✍️
Kinesthetic

Write answers by hand. Draw the agentic loop. Walk while reciting.

💬
Social

Explain to a study partner. Use the multiplayer mode. Debate answers.

🎬
Motion

Watch video explanations. Animate concepts in your head like a movie.

🗺️
Spatial

Memory palaces with real locations. Map concepts to physical places.
