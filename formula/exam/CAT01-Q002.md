# Exam Question CAT01-Q002

## Question
You are building a tutoring application and want Claude to provide guided hints rather than direct answers. What is the best architectural approach?

## Options
- A) Use a system prompt to define Claude's role and behavioral constraints.
- B) Set the temperature to 1.0 to ensure the answers are unpredictable.
- C) Programmatically truncate any response that looks like a final answer.
- D) Use a few-shot prompt with examples of direct answers only.

## Answer
A

## Explanation
System prompts are the primary way to define the model's persona, constraints, and behavior throughout a session.
