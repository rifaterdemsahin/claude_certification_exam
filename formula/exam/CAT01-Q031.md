# Exam Question CAT01-Q031

## Question
When evaluating prompt performance, why is it better to ask a Model Grader for "Reasoning" before the "Score"?

## Options
- A) It makes the API call cheaper.
- B) It forces the grader model to "think" through the evaluation, leading to more accurate scoring.
- C) It allows the grader to skip the scoring step if the reasoning is long enough.
- D) Reasoning is required by the Anthropic terms of service for all evaluations.

## Answer
B

## Explanation
Just like standard chain-of-thought, asking for reasoning first improves the quality of the final evaluation.
