# Memory Card MEM-Q001

## Question
What are the three stages of an Agentic Loop?

## Answer
Perceive, Think, and Act. The agent observes its environment, reasons about the observation, and takes action.

## Deep Dive
Agentic architecture focuses on the cyclic nature of AI interaction. By implementing loops, agents can self-correct and handle complex, multi-step tasks that linear prompts cannot.
